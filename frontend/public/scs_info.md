# **Sagehen Civic Scholars (SCS) Internship**

**Purpose**  
Launched in Fall 2024, the Sagehen Civic Scholars Internship is a distinctive program designed to connect Pomona College students with the City of Claremont’s local government. Open to students across all class years and academic disciplines, the program offers a hands-on opportunity to engage in meaningful civic work while exploring a wide range of departments and professional fields within municipal government.

The SCS internship involves projects ranging from areas like public policy, finance, marketing, communications, education, youth & senior programming… etc. Students will engage in fully in-person, hybrid, or remote projects and will be paired with a Project Liaision from the City Hall. Each internship cycle lasts for one semester, and applications open the semester before the internship cycle begins. 

By fostering collaboration between Pomona College and the City of Claremont, the internship not only equips students with valuable real-world experience and mentorship from city staff, but also helps strengthen the vital “town and gown” relationship. Interns benefit from a supportive and inclusive environment where they can build professional skills, contribute to community projects, and form lasting connections that bridge academic life and public service.

**Application information**

Applications for the Fall 2026 semester are open from **April 17th, 2026**, to **May 8th, 2026\.** The first round of applications will be a written portion, and the second round involves an interview with the City Hall Staff. The selection process is *not* on a rolling basis so all applications will be reviewed together after the final deadline. 

Contact the ASPC Director of Community Partnerships at \<communitypartnerships@aspc.pomona.edu\> for more information.
